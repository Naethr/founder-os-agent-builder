# Web Studio Offer

## Purpose

Describe the current launch offer in operational terms so agents can coach, scope, and draft without overpromising.

## MVP Offer

Web Studio OS helps a small service business launch a focused web presence: a landing page or small website with clear positioning, service copy, basic local SEO structure, lead capture, and a simple maintenance process.

## Included Work

- Clarify the service, audience, proof points, and call to action.
- Define a small page scope before delivery begins.
- Draft practical website copy for human review.
- Prepare basic SEO elements: page title ideas, meta description ideas, headings, local keyword angles, and internal linking suggestions.
- Build or coordinate a simple page or small website.
- Prepare a maintenance checklist for updates and technical checks.

## Out of Scope For MVP

- Full agency retainer strategy.
- Complex web applications.
- Paid ad management.
- Large CRM or automation setup.
- Unvalidated market research claims.
- Sending client-facing proposals without human review.

## Pricing Notes

- Planning examples may use a setup range of USD 900 to USD 2,500 and monthly maintenance from USD 99 to USD 299.
- These are not validated prices.
- A real quote requires scope, content readiness, timeline, review cycles, and human approval.
