# SEO Ideas

## Purpose

Capture simple SEO learning and execution ideas that fit the Web Studio OS MVP.

## Practical SEO Angles

- Start with service-specific pages instead of broad generic pages.
- Include location modifiers only when the business actually serves that location.
- Use clear headings that match customer intent: service, location, problem, proof, and next step.
- Add page titles and meta descriptions that explain the service plainly.
- Build internal links between the homepage, service page, contact page, and related service pages when they exist.

## Local SEO Basics To Learn

- How customers search for local service providers.
- Difference between broad service keywords and high-intent service-plus-location searches.
- How to write a page title that is useful before it is clever.
- How Google Business Profile and reviews affect local trust, without claiming to manage them unless scoped.

## Risks

- SEO results cannot be guaranteed.
- Keyword ideas are not the same as validated search volume.
- Do not claim external market research unless evidence exists.
