# Target Clients

## Purpose

Define the first practical client profiles for Web Studio OS without inventing real client data.

## Best-Fit Clients

- Local service businesses that rely on trust, clear explanations, and inbound leads.
- Freelancers, artisans, coaches, consultants, and small business owners with limited technical capacity.
- Businesses that need a focused website or landing page rather than a complex custom platform.
- Owners who can provide service details, proof elements, images, and basic decision feedback.

## Example Segments To Test

- Renovation contractors promoting one high-value service.
- Independent consultants clarifying a premium service offer.
- Coaches who need a credible page for discovery calls.
- Local wellness or home-service providers who need basic lead capture.

## Qualification Signals

- The prospect can describe the service they want to sell.
- The prospect has a realistic need for a small website, landing page, or service page.
- The prospect accepts a scoped MVP instead of asking for a large undefined platform.
- The prospect is willing to validate copy, offer details, and permissions before launch.

## Risks

- Some prospects may ask for full branding, ads, CRM, automation, and ongoing strategy before the core offer is validated.
- Real demand and pricing are still unvalidated; do not present assumptions as market facts.
