# Decisions

## Purpose

Track working decisions that guide the Web Studio OS MVP and prevent uncontrolled scope expansion.

## Current Decisions

- Use a local Markdown vault as the memory layer for this exercise.
- Use manual note reading and explicit citations instead of pretending to have semantic search.
- Keep the launch offer focused on landing pages and small service websites.
- Treat prices as planning examples only until validated with real prospects and delivery data.
- Require human validation before external, financial, public, permanent, or privacy-sensitive actions.
- Store evidence for agent runs in `evidence/runs/`.

## Product Boundaries

- Do not build a broad automation platform before the founder has validated demand.
- Do not claim real market research unless source notes or evidence exist.
- Do not store personal prospect data without validation and a clear reason.
- Do not let agents silently update permanent notes without review.

## Open Questions

- Which first niche should be tested: local contractors, coaches, consultants, or another service business segment?
- Which landing page package is easiest to deliver repeatedly?
- Which pricing range can be defended after real discovery calls?
