# Business Brief

## Purpose

Summarize the Web Studio OS business in a form that agents can use for planning, coaching, and grounded answers.

## Operating Context

- Web Studio OS is an AI-assisted web development micro-agency operating system.
- The MVP focus is helping small service businesses launch a clear web presence.
- The system should support practical delivery work: positioning, landing pages, local SEO basics, lead capture, sales replies, and maintenance.
- The business should stay narrow at launch instead of trying to become a full agency platform.

## Core Problem

- Small service businesses often know their trade but struggle to express a clear online offer.
- Their website problem usually includes weak positioning, unclear copy, poor lead capture, missing SEO basics, and no repeatable update process.
- A useful Founder OS agent should help the founder reduce ambiguity before promising price, scope, or timeline.

## Success Criteria

- The offer is simple enough to explain in one short paragraph.
- Delivery scope is documented before pricing or timeline commitments.
- Each client-facing action is reviewed by a human before it is sent or published.
- Evidence is stored for important runs and decisions.
