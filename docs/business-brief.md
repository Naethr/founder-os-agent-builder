# Business Brief

## Project Name

Founder OS Blueprint

## Project Case

This blueprint uses the default case: **Web Studio OS**, a small AI-assisted web development micro-agency operating system.

No existing personal project idea was documented in the repository before this blueprint was created.

## Target Customer

Web Studio OS targets freelancers, artisans, local businesses, coaches, consultants, and small service businesses that need a credible web presence but do not have the time, technical knowledge, or offer clarity to build it themselves.

Concrete example: a local renovation contractor wants to promote bathroom renovation services, capture qualified leads, and look more professional than competitors who rely only on social media pages or directory listings.

## Problem Solved

Small service businesses often know their trade but struggle to turn that expertise into a clear online offer. They need a website that explains what they do, proves credibility, captures leads, and can be maintained without becoming a technical burden.

Their main pain is not only "no website." It is a mix of unclear positioning, weak copy, poor lead capture, no SEO basics, and no repeatable maintenance process.

## Main Offer

Web Studio OS supports a focused service offer:

- Landing pages for specific services or campaigns.
- Small business websites with clear service pages.
- Basic local SEO structure.
- Lead capture forms and call-to-action flows.
- Maintenance checklists and content updates.
- AI-assisted research, copy drafts, implementation planning, and quality review.

The offer should stay narrow at MVP stage. A small, reliable website delivery process is more realistic than a broad agency platform.

## Fictional Pricing Model

Example pricing for planning only:

- Setup fee: USD 900 to USD 2,500 depending on page count, copy complexity, and content readiness.
- Monthly maintenance: USD 99 to USD 299 for updates, technical checks, basic SEO monitoring, and minor content changes.
- Optional add-ons: additional landing pages, advanced tracking setup, blog article preparation, and conversion review.

These prices are fictional and should be validated against the local market, delivery speed, and customer acquisition cost before use.

## Repetitive Tasks Founder OS Should Help Handle

- Analyze a prospect's business, audience, offer, and competitors.
- Prepare a landing page or small website scope.
- Draft service positioning and page copy.
- Generate an implementation checklist for the Code / Product Agent.
- Prepare SEO basics: page titles, meta descriptions, headings, local keywords, and internal linking ideas.
- Draft sales replies and follow-up emails.
- Prepare fictional quotes and invoice drafts for human review.
- Track client decisions, evidence, run notes, and reusable templates in the vault.
- Flag sensitive actions that require human validation.
