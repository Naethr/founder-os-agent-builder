## Output

Subject:
Re: Showcase website for your renovation services

Draft email:

Hi,

Thanks for the context. A showcase website for renovation services can be a good fit, but I would not give you a final price or timeline before checking the scope.

To estimate it properly, I need a few details:

- Which renovation service should the website promote first?
- Do you want one focused page or a small website with separate pages?
- Which town or service area should the site target?
- Do you already have photos, reviews, certifications, or project examples?
- Should visitors call, send a form, or request a quote?
- Is the text already written, or should I help structure and draft it?

Once these points are clear, I can prepare a small scope and a fictional quote draft for review. The goal would be to keep the first version focused, useful, and easy to validate before adding more pages.

Best,
[Founder Name]

Approval status:
Draft only. Human review is required before sending.

## Format Compliance

The output includes a subject, email body, missing scope questions, next step, and approval requirement.

## Observed Limits

The draft must not be sent without human approval. It does not include a final price, final deadline, guaranteed results, fake testimonials, or unsupported claims.
