## Output

Target segment hypotheses:

- Renovation contractors promoting one high-value service.
- Independent craft businesses with photos or proof but weak website structure.
- Small local service providers who need a focused landing page instead of a complex platform.

Fit signals:

- Clear primary service.
- Defined service area.
- Existing proof such as photos, reviews, certifications, or project examples.
- Willingness to validate copy and scope.
- Need for a simple contact action.

Disqualification signals:

- Request for a large undefined platform.
- Need for ads, CRM, automation, or full branding before the core offer is validated.
- No willingness to provide proof, content, or feedback.

Pain hypotheses:

- The business may have proof of work but no clear page structure.
- Visitors may not understand the exact service or location served.
- Contact flow may be unclear or inconsistent.

Outreach preparation:

- Prepare a manual discovery checklist.
- Draft a short message only after human review.
- Keep personal data collection minimal.
- Record only approved notes.

Approval requirement:
Human approval is required before collecting contact data, importing leads, sending outreach, or storing prospect information.

## Format Compliance

The output includes target segments, fit signals, pain hypotheses, outreach preparation, and approval requirements.
